About ZRI Strategies

Our mission is to empower organizations to scale, innovate, and lead meaningful social change. By combining innovative thinking with data-driven insight, we help you reimagine potential, build capacity, and deliver sustainable, lasting impact.

ZRI Strategies was founded by Zeenia Irani, a strategic leader with a track record of mobilizing millions in philanthropic and investment capital. Her expertise is built on leadership roles at FINCA International, Mercy Corps, and the United Nations Foundation. With an MBA from UC Berkeley's Haas School of Business, Zeenia brings a rigorous, cross-sector approach to solving the world's most pressing challenges.
