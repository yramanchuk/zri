About Us

At ZI Strategy Group, we team up with organizations that are passionate about their mission. We help them turn their big dreams into real, measurable progress. We're all about organizational transformation – using smart data and our deep understanding of different sectors to help you reach your full potential, build your strengths, and make a lasting difference.

Our goal is to give organizations the clear strategy they need to grow, the confidence to secure important funding, the knack for creating powerful partnerships that accelerate positive change, and the know-how to manage their finances effectively. We back all our advice with solid data, so every strategic move you make consistently delivers real results – turning those great ideas into undeniable successes.

The Founder

Zeenia Irani, founder of ZI Strategy Group, is a strategic advisor specializing in capital mobilization. She helps social impact and tech organizations secure funding and achieve measurable impact.

Zeenia has raised and directed millions in philanthropic and investment capital through leadership roles at FINCA International, Mercy Corps, the United Nations Foundation, and the World Food Programme. Her work across Latin America, Africa, and South Asia has built partnerships and scaled inclusive solutions.

She integrates AI and advanced data platforms into the nonprofit sector, bridging tech and social impact to develop effective, ethical, and inclusive tools.

Zeenia advises global institutions, governments, and Fortune 500 partners, driving systems-level change grounded in responsible AI and ethical innovation.

She holds an MBA from UC Berkeley’s Haas School of Business, specializing in social innovation and impact investing.
